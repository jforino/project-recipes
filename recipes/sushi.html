Sushi Recipe

Sushi is definitely one of my all time favorite foods. Though it can be a bit time consuming to make. It is totally worth it and can be really fun to make at parties. I have outlined an easy recipe for a California Roll below.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Sushi Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Imitation Crab
  • Sushi Grade Nori (Seaweed)
  • 1 Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame Seeds (optional) though it does increase the flavor dramatically.
  • Rolling Mat for Sushi

Steps

  1. Take your cooked sushi rice and toss with some rice vinegar and a little bit of sugar.
  2. Cut your cucumber into tiny slits.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a rolling mat
  4. Wet your hands and grab a handful of sushi rice, and place evenly on the sheet of nori.
  5. Add in imitation crab, sliced cucumbers, and avocado.
  6. Roll the nori sheet using the rolling mat.
  7. Sprinkle sesame seeds along the outside of the sushi roll.

    8. It is important to note that in order to mitigate rice sticking to your fingers, its best to have a bowl of water to dip your hands in once they start feeling sticky.

That's about it! Enjoy your sushi!
